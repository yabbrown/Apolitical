    World War I: The War That Changed It All

    World War I, often referred to as "The Great War," was a cataclysmic event that reshaped the political, social, and economic landscape of the world. Spanning from 1914 to 1918, the conflict involved major powers across Europe and beyond, leading to unprecedented levels of destruction and loss of life. While its origins lay in a web of alliances, militarism, and nationalism, its consequences were far-reaching, fundamentally altering the course of history.
